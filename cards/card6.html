Nainital

Nainital, a picturesque hill station in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, is known for its serene lakes, lush green hills, and pleasant climate. Located approximately 300 kilometers from Delhi, Nainital town is centered around the emerald-green Naini Lake, which offers boating opportunities against the majestic Himalayas. Visitors are drawn to Nainital for its scenic vistas, adventure activities, and charming colonial architecture. Being closer to Dehradun & Delhi, Nainital is a perfect weekend getaway. The key attractions of Nainital include the Naina Devi Temple, situated on the northern shore of Naini Lake, and the Snow View Point, accessible via a scenic cable car ride, offering panoramic views of the snow-clad peaks. The Mall Road, parallel to the lake, is perfect for strolls, shopping, and dining.
